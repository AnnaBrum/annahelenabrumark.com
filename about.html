I am a frontend developer with roots in design and textile art, where my practice focused on the dialogue between the analogue and the digital. Working hands-on with materials and exploring systems, patterns and iterations ultimately led me to code.

Today I combine my backgrounds in art, design and development to create responsive and maintainable interfaces, working with JavaScript, TypeScript and React. I also work across the full stack with Node.js, databases and backend integrations. I am especially interested in opportunities where I can bring together these two worlds of creative practice and technical development.
